1st Class Maths
A poll on the r/GCSE subreddit shows the change from Maths Genie to the YesGenie has not been received well.
These changes include not being able to do the following on YesGenie.com without an account registered with an email and name: downloading a past paper, viewing all questions in a question bank, view all of a revision guide. An AI chatbot has also been added to the website which many may dislike.
Most felt the changes were negative (voted "strongly disapprove" or "disapprove")
64.2% - 70/109
The most commonly option voted is "strongly disapprove"
45% - 49/109
A supermajority didn't feel positive about the changes (voted "strongly disapprove" or "disapprove" or "no contest")
88.1% - 95/109
A very small minority felt the changes to Maths Genie were positive
11.9% - 13/109
